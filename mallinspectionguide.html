Set-Up

Announce this in #session-announcements in the Stateview Discord:
:stateview: **Manditory Inspection** :stateview:
Host username: Mallarows
Host rank: **[-]**
Co-host username: -
Co-host rank: **[-]**
Helpers: -

Server ID: `!joinserver -`
Status: Unlocked :unlock:

h All staff Facility Director and under must come to the Sessions Room for a mandatory Inspection, failure to do so will result in a punishment.

Introduction

  • STS on the Host/Co-Host line

Host: Hello everyone! We will do a quick role call to start us off with this Inspection. When I call your username please say “Here, (Rank)!”

Once you finish role call, you may begin the Inspection.

Host: Hello, welcome to my Inspection! My username is Mallarows and my rank here at Stateview Corporation is -. My Co-Host will now introduce themselves.

Co-Host: Hello, I am Mallarows, my rank here at Stateview Corporation is -! I will be Co-Hosting this Inspection.

Host: Before we begin, let’s go over a few rules to help you pass this Inspection.

I - You must call me and my Co-Host by our ranks at all times.

II - Do not go AFK (Away From Keyboard) during this Inspection. Doing this will result in being staff warned and removed from this Inspection.

III - Do not say PTS (Permission To Speak) while I am speaking. Doing this will result in a warning, only use PTS if it’s an emergency.

IV - You must be respectful to everyone at all times. Not following this will result in a removal of the inspection, and a demotion/suspension.

V - If there is any problem, come to me, my Co-Host or the Helpers.

VII - No asking for promotions/ranks.

Host: Understood?

Host: Okay, we will now split you in two [II] groups, MRs (Chairperson+) and LRs (Warden-). I will be taking the (MRs/LRs).

Inspection Information

  • Take the MRs to either the Maximum and Solitary Cell Block or Yard.
  • Keep the MRs and LRs separated until Accuracy.
  • Once you get in the correct room, tell them to STS on the wall (example: “Everyone STS on the wall then we will begin.”)
  • Inspections are made up of 4 sections, Faces, Formations, Questions, and Accuracy.

Faces

Host:We will now do faces. Faces must be done, no excuses allowed. Faces are basically you facing the way the Host/Co-Host tells you.

Host: Please listen carefully as I will only explain each face once. DO NOT actually do the faces at this point.

Left Face - Turn left 90 degrees from the current position.

Right Face - Turn right 90 degrees from the current position.

Center Face - Turn to normal position. (Position from the beginning or face the Host/Co-Host)

About Face - 180 degrees from the current position.

Control Face - Face the Host/Co-Host at all times. (Host/Co-Host should be in the middle of your screen at all times) Note: Only Center Face breaks Control Face.

(MIDDLE-RANK-ONLY) Left Incline - Turn 45 degrees to the left from the current position.

(MIDDLE-RANK-ONLY) Right Incline - Turn 45 degrees to the right from the current position.

Once completed5 with Faces, you will proceed to the Formations phase.

Formations

  • Formations must be done, no excuses allowed.
  • The Host will be the Center for every formation, the Co-Host and Helpers will help put the trainees in the formations.

Host: We will now move on to Formations, these are team structured Formations, everyone must participate in it.

Host: Wedge Formation - The Host will be the head, all the trainees should form an arrow or a V formation like birds flying in the sky.

Host: Box Formation - The Host will be in the middle, all the trainees form a Box shape around them.

After you have finished Formations, you may proceed to Questions.

Questions

Host: Alright, I will now ask you a series of questions to test your grammar and knowledge.

PM the attendees 4 of the following questions:

  • What do you do in the situation of a lockdown?
  • Why is it important to have prison checkpoints?
  • What would you do if you saw someone abusing the votekick system?
  • What should you do if you see a cross-teamer?
  • What would you do if you saw a criminal?
  • What would you do if you saw an inmate passing the red hazard line?
  • What would you do if you saw someone AFK farming?
  • What would you do if you see someone abusing the control panel?
  • What would you do if you see someone breaking Roblox ToS?
  • What would you do if you saw a corrupt cop convincing people to break rules?
  • What is a riot shield used for?
  • What is a medical kit used for?

After you have finished Questions, move onto Accuracy.

Accuracy

  • Line up all attendees in the yard, by the prison wall.
  • Use one of your helpers to be the target, they will go back and forth beside the prison wall.
  • Host/Co-Host call the trainees up one by one, after they’re done with shooting tell them to sit on the bench.
  • Helper is required to sprint on the admin side. Helper should be walking on the non-admin side.

(IF NOT ALREADY IN THE YARD) Host: We will now be going to the Yard to do Accuracy. Make sure you have an X-26P (taser).

Host: We are now moving onto the accuracy phase! In this phase we will be testing you on your precision, aiming skills, listening skills and your accuracy!

Host: Your task here is simple, you will be called up one by one and your aim is to taze the moving target! You have 3 tries to hit the target. Please remain in a straight line until you are called up.

Host/Co-Host:
Come up.
Knock your taser!
Ready!
Aim!
Fire!

After attendees have taken their turn, have them take a seat on the benches.

Concluding the Inspection

  • When you’re done with Accuracy you will take all trainees to the benches.
  • In addition to the pictures taken during Formations, the host may choose to have all the attendees wedge as proof if they’d like.

Host: Please wait patiently as we discuss and rank the passers.

Once you have ranked the passers (if any), log the promotion(s) Announce the passers. Once you have announce them, end the inspection.

Host: Thank you for attending our Inspection today, it was a pleasure being your host!

Co-Host: Also from me, thank you for participating, dismissed!

h The Inspection is now over! Passers please say !rejoin to be able to see your new ranks! All dismissed from the session room!

Log your Inspection in #inspection-logs in intel and delete your session annoucement.
