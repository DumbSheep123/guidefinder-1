How the Riot works

Once you have finished with roles and dismissing staff, the host will announce the following: "h A Riot-Protocol will begin momentarily, please can no one attack each other! All staff please transport ALL inmates to the criminal base.”

Wait around 10-30 Seconds and Announce this:

h [RPC] During the RPC Wave 1 taking Inmates to the Criminal Base will not be seen as Cross-Teaming And you will not be punished.

Criminal Team Information

Only the Co-Host or the helpers will be set to go into the criminal team. They have full permission to use admin commands as a criminal. But they should add RPC (Crim Leader) at the end of their commands.

The Co-Host or helpers will be classified as Criminal Leaders (there is only allowed to be 1-2). Criminal Leaders will be in charge sorting out the criminals and starting and ending the wave.

Each wave will take 4-6 minutes, except the last wave will last till the Criminals win or the Officers win.

Main job of the Criminal team is to get a Criminal Leader to breach the 3 main areas of interest: Maximum Security Yard (MSY), Maximum and Solitary Cell Block (MCB), High Command Offices (HC).

If a Criminal Leader does breach one of these areas and has maintained to stay inside of that area for 10+ seconds, they should PM or DM the host.

If a main interest area gets breached, they do not need to breach it again for the next wave.

If all 3 main interests are breached, the criminal team wins!

If a Criminal Leader gets arrested, then they are out for the whole wave and cannot be saved.

Supervisors - Host or Co-host will be patrolling and supervising the officer team to see how they handle things in heated situations.

Once the Criminal Leader is ready, notify the host by PMing them notifying that you are ready to begin.

Host - After everyone is ready to begin the host will announce the waves

Each of these waves should only be 4-6 minutes long, 4 being the minimum 6 being the maximum except the last wave will last till the Criminals win or the Officers win.

h [STAFF] Just as a reminder NOT to arrest or cuff any Criminals during this protocol.

h Wave 1: Plan what you’re going to do. Captains talk with your post about your plan, Criminal Leader talk with your group about a plan.

Wait 4-6 Mins before you announce the next Wave!

h Wave 2 : Attack each other and get all the backup you need!

Wait 4-6 Mins before you announce the next Wave!

h Wave 3: Breach attack each others bases, NO CAMPING. You can arrest criminals and take them to solitary/max.

Once all waves have been completed, conclude the Protocol.
