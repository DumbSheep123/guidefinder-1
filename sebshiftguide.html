Introduction

  • STS on the Host/Co-Host line

Host: Hello, welcome to my Shift! My username is Sebwoodhead and my rank here at Stateview Correctional Facility is Prison Commissioner. My Co-Host will now introduce themselves.

Co-Host: Hello, I am Sebwoodhead and my rank here at Stateview Correctional Facility is Prison Commissioner. I will be Co-Hosting today's Shift!

Host: Before we begin, let’s go over a few rules to help you pass this training!

I - You must call me and my Co-Host by our ranks at all times.

II - Do not go AFK (Away From Keyboard) or say PTS (Permission To Speak) while I am speaking, during this training. Doing this will result in removal.

III - You must be respectful to everyone at all times. Not following this will result in a removal of the training and a demotion/suspension.

IV - If there is any problem, come to me, my Co-Host or any other CP+ assistance. You must not ask us for a promotion/rank or it be instant removal.

VI – PTS is active unless stated otherwise. Talking without permission WILL result in a strike.

Host: Understood?

Roles

Host: My co-host will now hand out roles; I will explain them after.

Role explanations:

Patrol Team - Patrols the prison, follows the status.

Scout Team - Patrols outside the prison and guards the prison from watch towers.

Response Team - Guards around the set status, answers to any panic calls, watches over checkpoints and control rooms.

Camera Operator - Works on cameras, reporting about the prison on radio.

Captain - Head of a group of people, keeping control in their group and restoring order.

Host: h Shift | Dismissed to roles, stay back if you have any questions.

Host/Co-host: h Please do not change the Status or mess around with lockdown due to an on-going shift, failure to do so will result in a punishment.

Concluding the Shift

Host: h Can everyone from the Shift please come back to the Sessions room.

Once you have called the attendees back, take a picture for the log and announce the hardest workers.

Host: Time for the hardest workers: (announce them)

Host: Thank you for attending my Shift today. I would like to say that it has been a pleasure to be your host today!

Co-Host: Also from me, thank you for participating in our shift, dismissed!

h Shift | Dismissed, have a great day/night.
